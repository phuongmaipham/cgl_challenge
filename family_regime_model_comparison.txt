Universe
--------
Products: CORN, SOYABEAN, WHEAT_SRW, WHEAT_HRW.

No external data was fetched in this experiment.

Strategy Definitions
--------------------
- avg_all_signals: Simple average of every provided economic signal.
  Formula: mean(price, curve, COT, public physical, Cargill inventory, Cargill crush signals)
  Economic rationale: Broad diversification; assumes weak signals are safer in a simple basket.
  Overfit note: Low model overfit, but can be economically diluted.
- equal_family_weight: Average within each family, then equal weight each family.
  Formula: mean(price_family, curve_family, cot_family, public_physical_family, cargill_physical_family)
  Economic rationale: Prevents a family with many columns from dominating the signal.
  Overfit note: Low model overfit; family choices are researcher-defined.
- ic_family_selected: Keep families with train IC above threshold, select highest validation IC family.
  Formula: selected_family_signal, oriented by train IC sign
  Economic rationale: Use only families with evidence of predictive rank correlation.
  Overfit note: Moderate/high selection risk; validation IC can be noisy.
- regime_best_family_trend_mr: Trend/MR observable regime; pick best validation-IC family separately per regime.
  Formula: 1(trend)*best_trend_family + 1(MR/chop)*best_mr_family
  Economic rationale: Signals may work in different states; wheat/corn can trend while soy can revert.
  Overfit note: Moderate selection risk; weights are fixed masks, not optimized.
- regime_avg_families_trend_mr: Trend/MR observable regime; average all IC-passing families in each regime.
  Formula: 1(trend)*avg(IC-passing trend families) + 1(MR/chop)*avg(IC-passing MR families)
  Economic rationale: Keeps regime conditioning but avoids picking a single family winner.
  Overfit note: Lower than best-family regime selection, but still regime-research risk.
- ols_kalman_filter: Dynamic linear OLS coefficients updated with a Kalman filter.
  Formula: y_t = x_t beta_t + error; beta_t follows a random walk
  Economic rationale: Allows relationships to drift through time without a full static fit.
  Overfit note: Coefficient-estimation risk; parameters are fixed, not OOS tuned.
- ridge_expanding_alpha100: Expanding-window Ridge with fixed alpha 100.
  Formula: argmin ||y-Xb||^2 + 100||b||^2, refit every 21 trading days
  Economic rationale: Regularized linear combination of all signals.
  Overfit note: Coefficient-estimation risk; fixed alpha reduces but does not remove it.
